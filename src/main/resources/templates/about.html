Neighborhood Assurance

About Neighborhood Assurance

Neighborhood Assurance was developed and released in the first half of 2020. The founders include Claire Church, Michael Lawson, Tad Preston, and Madison Rosas.


This application allows you to make a search for crime data by inputting the state and the a range of years. This will show you the crime statistics of that state in within that range of time, helping you to determine if the state is safe for you and your family.
